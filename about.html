Hello, welcome to moodcalls.com! This website was made to supplement the visual novel by the name of "Mood Calls" which was created using the program renpy. In this visual novel, we tell the story of a group of teens who navigate their way through their high school by joining a club which supports them through all of their mental health and life challenges. we decided to pursue this project as our Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program final project because we beleive in the importance of raising awareness of mental health and mental illness in order combat the stigma associated with discussing them and to also provide those who may be suffering from their own mental health a sense of comfort and understanding. We hope you enjoy our story!
